Amanraj Gill, born on November 1, 1998, began his musical journey at 15 and quickly rose to prominence as a Haryanvi singer and music producer. His tireless dedication and hard work fuel his success, while his soft-spoken and down-to-earth nature endears him to fans worldwide. His music captures the vivid spirit of Haryana through hits like "Tag Awara Ke", "Kale Kagaz", "Beretta" and "Gaddi Pachhe Gaddi", blending traditional beats with modern flair.
